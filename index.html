LORD CUPID

Lord Cupid (real name) Akintade Adewale Desmond was born on May 30, hails from Ekiti State, south west part of Nigeria. His genres are R&b, Afro Pop and soul. He is made up of striking vocal and explanatory lyrics,he has uniqueness in his style of writing and composing and his electrifying fusion and swap between Genres shows his musical versatility. Lord Cupid has always had a flair for music from childhood,his passion for music made him join the church choir at age seven, and he is fond of writing poem and short stories while growing up that got him the name “Cupid” meaning “god of love”while in secondary school. Lord Cupid started music professionally in 2005,with a group called ‘Heartthrobs‘ He recorded his first solo song titled “What i got” in 2008, the song was produced by”Fliptyce” currently one of the best music producer in Nigeria. Lord Cupid cited Micheal Jackson, Bob Marley and R. Kelly has his biggest musical influences though has huge admiration for Trey Songz and The Weeknd and Drake. Lord Cupid is presently working on his Debut EP “The Electrifier”
